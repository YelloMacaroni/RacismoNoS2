The Story



It's a Sunday night, late at night and you forgot
to push your project which is due to the next day,
so you go to EPITA with the aim of finally pushing
this project, however, not everything is going as
expected, the malevolent spirit of an acdc that
would have disappeared a long time ago haunted the
place for a long time. The objective is to reach the
third floor while escaping the ghost (not so ghost)
in order to push his project. To do this, you will
have to succeed in overcoming the various obstacles,
solving puzzles and find the key objects to be able
to climb from floor to floor while avoiding this forgotten ACDC
